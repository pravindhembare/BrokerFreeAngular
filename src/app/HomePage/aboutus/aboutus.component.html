About Us

Introduction

BrokerFree is a new home rental platform that makes it easier to find your new home without paying any brokerage. Use our verified listing to ensure you find your dream home by contacting the owners directly. In case you cannot find a property you are looking for, please post a requirement and we'll send you email notification with properties matching your requirements.

How we can help

Brokerage rental platform lets you find tenants without any broker acting as a middleman. Apart from the fact that this can be much more convenient and safe. Also, you end up saving on brokerage that is anywhere between 15 days to 2 month's rent. When both you and tenant are not paying the brokerage, tenants are also willing to pay a little higher rent. So, you can expect to get 2-4% higher rent as compared to what brokers may get you. It's a win-win for both the landlord and tenant.
